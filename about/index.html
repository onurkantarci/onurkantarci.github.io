About Me

Hello! I'm Onur Kantarci, a passionate and dedicated full-stack developer from Prizren, Kosovo. With a background in law and a self-taught journey into the world of web development, I have developed a diverse skill set that allows me to tackle complex projects with creativity and precision.

My journey in tech began with a deep curiosity and drive to create impactful digital solutions. Over the past few years, I have honed my skills in various technologies, including Vue, React, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and Python. My expertise extends to building robust backend systems with Express.js, managing databases with MongoDB and PostgreSQL, and containerizing applications with Docker for seamless deployment.

I've had the privilege of learning from a senior full-stack developer and being mentored by my brother, which has significantly shaped my approach to development. I enjoy taking on freelance projects where I can apply my knowledge and continue to grow.

Some of my notable projects include a comprehensive Vue-Express Docker setup, a Python-based task scheduling system, a secure Express.js application with DDoS protection, and a variety of web applications featuring authentication, data management, and real-time communication.
